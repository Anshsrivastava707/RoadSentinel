About This System

A national initiative to transform urban road maintenance through data-driven decision making.

Our Mission

To reduce road damage recurrence by 40% across Indian cities by 2030 through predictive analytics, proactive maintenance, and transparent infrastructure management.

System Overview

The Urban Road Damage & Pothole Recurrence Prediction System is a national digital infrastructure platform developed under the Smart Cities Mission and AMRUT 2.0 initiatives. It integrates real-time data from multiple sources to provide municipal engineers and urban planners with actionable intelligence for optimizing road maintenance budgets and improving citizen safety.
